Risk Engine Deep Dive

How the Rust contract scores risk

💱

Price Impact

How much the swap moves the pool price. Large trades on thin liquidity = high impact = high risk.

📈

Moving Average (MA20)

Tracks last 20 trade prices per pool. Detects deviation from fair price. Spots manipulation in real time.

📊

Volatility Tracking

Rolling standard deviation across recent trades. High volatility = higher risk score. Adapts to market conditions.

🎯

Scoring System

Score 0-100 based on price impact, MA20 deviation, and volatility.
GREEN <40 · YELLOW <70 · RED ≥70
