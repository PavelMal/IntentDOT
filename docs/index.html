What makes IntentDOT special

🛡

AI Risk Guardian

Every transaction scored GREEN / YELLOW / RED. High risk = blocked. Evaluates slippage, liquidity depth, pool drain.

💬

Natural Language

No forms, no dropdowns. Just type "Swap 10 DOT to USDT" or "Send 50 USDC to 0xABC...". AI understands.

✨

Token Factory

"Create a token called PEPE with 1M supply" → ERC-20 deployed, minted, auto-whitelisted. All from chat.

On-Chain Whitelist
