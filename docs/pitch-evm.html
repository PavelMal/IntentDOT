Killer Features

What makes IntentDOT special

💬

Natural Language DeFi

4 intent types: swap, transfer, create token, check balance — all from one chat interface.

🛡

AI Risk Guardian

AI pre-scores every trade before you confirm. GREEN / YELLOW / RED risk levels protect you from bad trades.

💸

Token Transfers

"Send 50 USDT to 0x..." — transfer any ERC-20 token to any address, all from natural language.

✨

Token Factory

Create ERC-20 tokens from chat. Auto-whitelisted for trading on the built-in DEX.

📊

Live Portfolio & Markets

Real-time balances, pool reserves, sparkline price charts, 12s auto-refresh from chain data.

📜

On-Chain Transaction History

Full history from blockchain events + Blockscout API. Swaps, transfers, token creates — all on-chain.

Claude AI evaluates slippage, liquidity depth, price impact, and pool drain ratio before you confirm. Every trade gets a risk score with clear GREEN / YELLOW / RED verdict. High-risk trades are blocked automatically.
